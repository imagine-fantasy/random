Nexus · Cross-Team Intelligence

Nexus reads across all squad memories.
Surfaces what no single team can see.

Example 1 · Duplication Caught Early

Two squads build the same thing. Stopped before it starts.

Squad B drops phone validation into next sprint. Nexus knows Squad A built one 2 sprints ago. Both POs alerted before sprint commits. Zero wasted engineering days.

Example 2 · Hidden Dependency Surfaced

Integration risk caught in Sprint 1, not Sprint 4

Squad B builds merchant onboarding. Nexus detects this needs Squad A's payment validator downstream. Both teams aligned in Sprint 1, not when integration breaks in Sprint 4.

Both happen before damage is done. Multiplied across 30+ squads, this is where the strategic value compounds.
